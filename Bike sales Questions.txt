We have a data of bike sales which include 3 tables sales table , product table and customers table.

Problem Statement-Create 2 reports to find important metrics and behaviors-

1) Customer Report

Purpose- This report consolidates key customer metrics and behaviors

Highlights:
 i). Gather essential fields such as names, ages, and transaction details.
 ii). Segment customers into categories (VIP, Regular, New) and age groups.
 iii). Aggregate customer-level metrics:
	- total orders
	- total sales
	- total quantity purchased
	- total products
	- lifespan (in months)
 iv). Calculate valuable KPIs:
	- recency (months since last order)
	- average order value
	- average monthly spend

2)Product Report

Purpose- This report consolidates key product metrics and behaviors.

Highlights:
i). Gather essential fields such as product name, category, subcategory, and cost.
ii). Segment products by revenue to identify High-Performers, Mid-Range, or Low-Performers.
iii). Aggregate product-level metrics:
       - total orders
       - total sales
       - total quantity sold
       - total customers (unique)
       - lifespan (in months)
iv). Calculate valuable KPIs:
       - recency (months since last sale)
       - average order revenue (AOR)
       - average monthly revenue
